About J. Nathalie

Core

I’m a curious and imaginative designer who enjoys soft colours, cute visuals, and playful ideas. I like creating designs that feel friendly, simple, and easy to understand.

Work

I work across design, interactive media, and 3D. I enjoy building clear and simple layouts, adding playful touches, and bringing ideas to life through motion and hands-on making. I like creating work that looks clean, feels easy to use, and has a bit of character.

Love

I enjoy hobbies that help me relax and stay creative. I like swimming, gaming, crafting, traveling, and spending time outdoors. These activities inspire me and keep my ideas fresh.
